Aelthar was born in southern Himari to a family of farmers\. in his younger years the family noticed that his skin turned darker with time and his eyes changed from blue to the radiant orange they now have\. They were distressed at first but after having been assured by an apothecary they realized he was simply dragonmarked like some of his ancestors\.

<a id="_2pvon87sjgiz"></a>The Doryu Family:

The Doryu family was originally a smaller branch of a dragonmarked noble family\. The family had a reputation for their strong dragonmarked summons but with time the family got less and less dragonmarked members until the family was eventually lost to the times\. They now live as simple farmers with the story of their family being passed on through stories or a single brooch with the family Sigil \(a wave made of shadows\)\.

__Recent times:__

Aelthar went into work as a bounty hunter using his magical capabilities and Nocturne to create quite the reputation for himself\. Once he had recognition he got requested for certain jobs like protecting politicians and taking down syndicates\. He mostly worked alone and sent a large part of his money to his family who now lives better than before\. Eventually he got recruited into the myridon army for a large sum of money that was sent to his family located in Himari regardless of the standing between the 2 nations as of now\.
