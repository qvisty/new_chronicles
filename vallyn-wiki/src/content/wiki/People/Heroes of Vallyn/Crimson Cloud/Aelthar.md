Personality Traits
------------------

Despite Aelthar's cold demeanour he frequently jokes around, mostly at others expense, but jokes nonetheless. Aelthar is usually very calculating but becomes very rash when he is emotional. Aelthar doesn't drink alcohol, but loves tea and of course milk.

Background
----------

Aelthar grew up on a small farm in southern Himari and eventually showed signos of his dragonmark. After he was given his first summon(Nocturne) he left to become a bounty hunter. Aelthar worked all over the fallen empire and had a run in with Alexei while working one of his jobs with khalia. Eventually the Myridon forces reached out to hire him in their new military campaign against the Stormwatch duchy. There he met the rest of what now is know as The Crimson Cloud.
