He is outgoing and loud. He seems to only really connect with his grep, Sokoya. He is a very go-with-the-flow individual but kind and just wants to help people out.

Background
----------

He was a smart child to a human father and drow mother. His father was an archeologist who went missing and therefore his mother went after him when Imolin was old enough to take care of herself. He then had his care transferred to his aunt, Khalia's mother. He went to a school to become an entertainer, he eventually pissed off one of his clients who cursed him with visions. He became a recluse until he went to the surface to help the Crimson cloud with their attacks.
