Background
----------

Expand

Not much is known about Alexei's early life, not even her surname. She is an infamous agent and enforcer of the Bestial Enclave, an incredibly powerful crime guild whose members keep their identities hidden by adopting an animal persona and wearing an animal mask, led by a stout individual known only as The Boar. She was captured some years ago, unwilling to impart even the slightest details about her organization no matter what interrogation methods were used. She was offered a taste of freedom if she works for the Myridon army.
