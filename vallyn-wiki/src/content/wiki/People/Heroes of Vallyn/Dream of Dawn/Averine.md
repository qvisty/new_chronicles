Personality Traits
------------------

Averine is a cheerful and good meaning person, but can some times come across as to direct. He is a carefree soul both because that is the way he is and a sprinkle of ignorance of certain ways of the world.

Background
----------

Averine Was born in The Republic of Satrea only a couple of weeks before the Dusk War began and was taken to the Luminara Beacon for refuge alongside his sister. He spent the remainder of the Dusk war within the beacon. In the aftermath he and his sister remained within the beacon. The sister became a researcher with time and Averine was taught by several of the orders within the beacon for the next 2 decades. Averine eventually left the beacon and the age of 28 and found his way to regnum aurelia after a couple of weeks.
