Personality Traits
------------------

Gabrielle was never great at talking about his feelings. Believing that only Morric could ever really understand him and his thoughts, he now feels emotionally numb and doesn't trust himself to communicate well with others. That's why he'd rather stay cold and straight to the point when he talks. Making use of his military training to push emotional connections aside, Gabrielle tries to think 5 steps ahead in every situation. He takes care of himself, tries to look well put together, even though he doesn't feel like it.

Gabrielle doubts himself, fearing that his father was right and that he will never be good enough for the tasks in front of him. Now he feels constantly on edge and expects a wrong turn of events around every corner. He doesn't know what he'd do if he loses one more person important to him or if he finds himself unworthy of the trust that Soren and Morric have put in him.

Background
----------

Back home, Gabrielle and Morric were responsible for a defeat of the Woodelves when they were defending their forests. They are responsible for the death of thousands and knowing that their name and reputation will forever be tarnished by that, Gabrielle gave up on ever redeeming himself in the eyes of his people. While he always reacted with offense when he was accused and reminded of what he did by the woodelves, or by his father, deep down he always knew that they were right to treat him badly. Gabrielle was jealous of Morric still being able to put so much effort into redeeming himself, but never found it in himself to join him. Leaving their home was a relief for him, but it was only the beginning to Gabrielle feeling like he completely lost his way.
