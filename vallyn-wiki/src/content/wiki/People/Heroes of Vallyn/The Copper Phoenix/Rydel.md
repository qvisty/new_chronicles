Description / Appearance
------------------------

_Rydel is a young half-elf, with an athletic build. He’s a bit shorter than the average height, however, stronger than the average due to helping his uncle on the farm, or in his smith. His hair is silver, and his skin tanned. Across his arm and shoulder are various crude tattoos, some just patterns, while other bearing draconic resemblance. Down his back runs his dragonmark - a symbol covering his spine, made to resemble the spine of a dragon, and to the trained eye, a copper one specifically._

_He carries around a mask forged of copper, with draconic imagery upon it. Forged by his uncle, and bearing the abbreviation AC - Alwyn Caldwell. Formerly, he also carried two scimitars and a glaive, all forged by his uncle, but he has since switched them out for a magical glaive, its blade resembling a dragon's head._

Personality Traits
------------------

Rydel tries as much as possible to be good person as he can. He wants to make his uncle proud of him.

He is very protective of his friends, and is generally a quiet type of person. In many ways, he is probably the counterpart to Kani.

Since the fight at the Goblin fort near Haverton, his mood and attitude had been slowly reclining, and he hit an especially dark period immediatly after Kani died. He also acted recklessly, but after being brought back, he learned that was due to darkvein. He now is on an upwards slope, in a big part due to support from Canela, and a want to be strong for the group.

Background
----------

Rydel came from the small village of Llyne in the Unclaimed Lands, where he was raised by his uncle, Alwyn. Recently before starting his adventure, his uncle passed away, and Rydel came into possession of a dragon egg. He traveled west from Llyne, where he and Syphax, his dragon, ended up captured by pirates.

He has later revealed that he had befriended an adult Copper Dragon near his village, which is where he had his first Dragon Companion. Nirlilah, as her name was, was Syphax' mother.

He also revealed that the reason he left Llyne was that The Stalkers, following a Red Shadow Dragon, had killed Nirlilah and Alwyn, his uncle.

Recently, a lot of his family history, which was previously unknown to him, has been revealed. He has a brother, Aurelius, and through their relation with Varo, they are technically princes in Setmerun - their father, Varo, being cousin to Emperor Aranian.
