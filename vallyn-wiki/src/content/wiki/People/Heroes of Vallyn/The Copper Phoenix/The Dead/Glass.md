Personality Traits
------------------

Glass has a child-like personality. His now consciousness is only one year old and is very unexperienced when it comes to a lot of things. Small shiny trinkets are a vice for him and he gets very easily distracted by them. His sticky fingers tend to put him in uncomfortable situations but till now he somehow always found a way out of them again. Sometimes he drops a bit of knowledge tid bits that he picked up in some book or another, but it’s not clear if he really understands what he’s saying himself most of the time.

Background
----------

Glass came to himself barely a year ago. His metal body is a bit older than that tho, not that he remembers any of that time. Sometimes he has something like a vision or a dream that allows him to dip into abilities he didn’t know he had before.
