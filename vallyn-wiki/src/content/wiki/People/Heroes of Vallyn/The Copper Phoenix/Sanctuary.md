Description / Appearance
------------------------

_Short with horns extending well past her head, bone white and shaped almost like vertebrae on a spine, she had long white wavy hair, half tied into a braid on the back of her head with white pupilless eyes. Blood red skin decorated with glittering white tattoos on her upper chest and shoulders. She wears what seems to be a black toga-like dress with a sheer cape that covers most of her shoulders._

**HAIR****: White**

Personality Traits
------------------

*   Attentive to her surroundings
    
*   Smiles, often disturbingly
    
*   Counts done on her fingers, constantly restarting when something bothers her
    
*   She plays with the needle and chain in her horns
    
*   Cautious and untrusting towards new people
    
*   Suspicious of goodwill from others
    

Background

Sanctuary was born into a high-class family in Liberty, she had studied religion in the past and has since left the nation to explore. She ran away from Liberty, to get away from her family and her ex-fiancee, Casris Ancus of the Sorio family.
