_Kani is a tiefling with gray skin and brown blackish short hair. The green of her pupils are reminiscent of the jade earrings she used to wear in her original body. She wears black and white comfortable clothes with softer fabrics. The only other color on her are the red strings she tied around her horns._

Personality Traits
------------------

Kani's body still feels very new to her, with her memories jumbled all together she tries to string them apart piece by piece and sometimes the memories of what her body used to feel like and used to be able to do makes her become clumsy and unsure. She has more patience than she did before though, she wants to make the best out of the second chance she got and that makes her feel disconnected to her previous life sometimes. Like it was more a dream than reality. Kani wants to be good, and to be a good friend to Rydel and the others.

Background
----------

Kani is convinced that the second chance she was granted by a powerful god, is for her to fulfill her purpose. They were meant to save the world, they are meant to be heroes and she will fight the right fight to fulfill her destiny.
