Description / Appearance
------------------------

_He’s a broad shouldered, pillar of a man. His wide frame and large calloused hands give the impression of an intimidating brute. Though the second you look into his deep blue eyes it’s plain to see that he’s a gentle giant. No grizzled war veteran would be seen fiddling with a scrap of metal and eyes so distant they can only see the past, and any potentially interesting contraptions. Sometimes the man seems so lost in thought it seems that the floating orb always near his head sees for him._

_It’s often hidden under his armor but when he does occasionally take it off it becomes much more apparent that he’s a giant (but friendly) tool._

_A large winking smiley face could be seen on his right bicep amidst all the other tattoos that pepper his upper torso. It’s the one he likes the most, and the one he will get very, very angry over it being made fun of._

**HAIR****: Dark blue, short and unkempt**

**EYES****: Blue**

**SKIN****: Light Green**

Personality Traits
------------------

Johan is a friendly but awkward introvert, he’ll greet the way that’s socially acceptable, but if he doesn’t have any immediate interest in the person he’s speaking to he’ll remain polite, but quiet. Unless he has some ale in him, then everything is funny to him, even a metal pipe falling.

What brings out the enthusiastic chatterbox in Johan is craftsmanship. A unique and intricately crafted trinket, a masterfully designed sword, shield or armor will catch his eye from any distance. He’ll go from a stoic brick wall to an excited child, rambling endlessly about the marvel that is craftsmanship, be it a magical amulet made of dragon scales or a simple steel sword with a masterful engraving. As long as it separates itself from the mundane, Johan will bond himself to the object faster than any interpersonal bond.

He has a slightly deeper than average voice, though when he gets excited it rises a few octaves and his reasonable speaking pace accelerates to incomprehensible gibberish, be it in common or orcish, especially in orcish since he has a heavy common accent. Heavy enough to be noticed by native speakers, which is why he doesn’t speak it often.

Background
----------

Johan was born to a family of extremely talented and hardworking craftsmen.

His dream as a kid was to travel the world, showing off how amazing his family’s work is and make ever greater things.

Sometimes, dreams stay dreams and the extraordinary aren’t exceptions to the rule.
