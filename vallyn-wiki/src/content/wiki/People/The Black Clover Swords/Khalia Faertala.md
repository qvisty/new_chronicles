**Full name and title**
=======================

> _“Let's hope this goes better than that job in Myria, ay?”_ \- Khalia, talking to Aelthar

Personality
-----------

Khalia is an assassin, and has the personality one would almost expect from one - she puts on sarcasm in standard conversation, with a hint of cockyness, and never shows any deeper emotions.

When on a mission, or in a fight, she likes to "play with her food", in a sense. Especially if she knows she has the upper hand, she'll even prolong the fighting to see the target suffers longer. She doesn't mind seeing others suffering, and she doesn't bother hiding that.

* * *

Background
----------

Khalia doesn't like to share much of her story, or where she came from, or anything like that. Through various sources, you've heard she was exiled from her home in the Underdark, and you know she's been working as an assassin ever since arriving in Myridon.

She has also worked with Aelthar on multiple missions before.

**STATUS:** Deceased

**DISPOSITION:** Deceptive, Malicious, Cocky

**RACE:** Drow

---------------------

*   Is controlling a majority of the cultists following the Black Clover Swords in the Stormwatch Duchy.
    
*   Is very hard to lock down in fights, using her teleporting abilities to strike her target before teleporting away again.

* * *

Appearance
----------

Khalia is a drow elf. She has purple skin and long, white hair, which she generally wears not tied up in any way. She has black armor with golden trims, and her Ruby Longsword match her blood red eyes.

Background
----------

Count Strahd von Zarovich is the ruler of all the lands of Barovia. He rules with an iron fist, relying on fear and mysticism to keep the Barovians in line.  
He has been in power for centuries, never wavering, and with very few revolts against him.  
Some call him the Devil, others Master. All are afraid of him.

Appearance
----------

Strahd is pale, looking to be in his forties.  
His jet-black hair is kept swept back from his face, resulting in a prominent brow and widow’s peak.  
His features are gaunt but handsome and his dark red eyes have an intensity that can make even the strongest man nervous.  
His physique is lean but muscular, and he has been noted for his height—well over six feet.  
He tends to dress in exquisite finery, albeit in a somewhat outdated style.
