Personality
-----------

Meiavor is a quiet man, usually keeping to himself instead of engaging too much in conversation. However, when necessary, Meiavor can become both extremely persuasive and manipulative, and very often seems to enjoy doing stuff like this, especially if to a maddening extend on whoever he is currently targeting.

Background
----------

Count Strahd von Zarovich is the ruler of all the lands of Barovia. He rules with an iron fist, relying on fear and mysticism to keep the Barovians in line.  
He has been in power for centuries, never wavering, and with very few revolts against him.  
Some call him the Devil, others Master. All are afraid of him.

Appearance
----------

Strahd is pale, looking to be in his forties.  
His jet-black hair is kept swept back from his face, resulting in a prominent brow and widow’s peak.  
His features are gaunt but handsome and his dark red eyes have an intensity that can make even the strongest man nervous.  
His physique is lean but muscular, and he has been noted for his height—well over six feet.  
He tends to dress in exquisite finery, albeit in a somewhat outdated style.
