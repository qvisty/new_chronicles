Personality
-----------

Daephne is playful and mischievous. She likes to play tricks on others, and doesn't take things too seriously. However, while she does like to play pranks on others, quite often she won't really care too much about what they do to the receiving end, and will only really care about her own fun.

She often acts in a childish way, but can switch around in an instant, turning as serious as ever, and coupled with her use of illusion magic, it can turn scary fast.

* * *

Background
----------

Not much is really known about Daephne's background. As a young girl, she was an urchin, living on the streets of Myrida Aeca. At some point, Selwyn found her and took her in, basically becoming like an older sibling or mentor to her. Since, she's been loyal to him and the Black Clover Swords.

Appearance
----------

Strahd is pale, looking to be in his forties.  
His jet-black hair is kept swept back from his face, resulting in a prominent brow and widow’s peak.

His physique is lean but muscular, and he has been noted for his height—well over six feet.  
He tends to dress in exquisite finery, albeit in a somewhat outdated style.
