Personality
-----------

Harman is a very good example of how looks can be deceiving. Looking past his intimidating appearance, you'd find that Harman is a very kind man. He always tries his best to be polite, and looks after everyone the best he can, and makes sure everyone is alright.

While he is genuine and kind man outside of combat, he is able to tap into a rage that dwells deep within him to muster the sheer power or will to withstand beatings no man should be able to. What allows him to do this, you're not quite sure.

* * *

Background
----------

In his early days, Harman was a soldier for The Myridon Dynasty before he became a zealot, leaving the army to become a guard at the religious order where the Black Clover Swords all stem from.

Here, he quickly rose through the ranks to become leader of the guards, before being invited to the Black Clover Swords by Selwyn.

Appearance
----------

Harman is a tall man, bordering 7 feet and easily towering the rest of the Black Clover Swords. He has a quite intimidating look, as over his years of fighting, especially fighting so recklessly as he does, he has acquired a great deal of scars that cover his face and body, even losing him an eye.

Count Strahd von Zarovich is the ruler of all the lands of Barovia. He rules with an iron fist, relying on fear and mysticism to keep the Barovians in line.  
He has been in power for centuries, never wavering, and with very few revolts against him.  
Some call him the Devil, others Master. All are afraid of him.

Appearance
----------

Strahd is pale, looking to be in his forties.  
His jet-black hair is kept swept back from his face, resulting in a prominent brow and widow’s peak.

His physique is lean but muscular, and he has been noted for his height—well over six feet.  
He tends to dress in exquisite finery, albeit in a somewhat outdated style.
