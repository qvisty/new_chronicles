**Magnus Ryiad Redgrave**
=========================

> _**“Let’s call it a show of good faith”**_ _\- Magnus_

Personality
-----------

Magnus is a narcissist, who doesn’t consider the wants or feelings of those around him. He seems to be at his best when actively putting others down, be it through insults or acts of violence.

In spite of his rougher side, he still retains the bearing of a noble and some of their qualities. He can be formal, knows how to lead, and remains honest. If the situation demands it, he can prove to be rather charismatic

* * *

Background
----------

Magnus is the firstborn son of Earl Donra Redgrave of Epithon. He belongs to a family very well renowned for their magical capabilities.

Appearance
----------

Magnus has a light skin tone. His Jet-Black hair looks messy and unkempt, often covering both his eyes and ears. His features are clearly youthful, yet handsome, with his eyes being an intense bright red colour, clearly visible even in dark environments

His physique is lean, but clearly muscular. He towers over the average person, with a height well over 6 feet. He likes to dress in traditional light clothes from his homeland. Over those, he wears a warm, red, fur-collared cloak, with his family insignia on the back.
