The Restoration of the Ridian Empire
====================================

**As decreed by His Imperial Majesty, Metilles the Reforger**

**Year 960 A.D. of the Myrian Calendar**

After a decade of chaos, war, and division, the Fallen Empire has been reforged - not in the image of conquest, but in the spirit of unity and survival, and standing together in a time where the people of the world need it most.

Emperor Metilles, a once thought weak claimant to the throne, showed his true resolve in uniting the people. First leading them against the demons of the abyss, and then turning home to end the War of False Emperors, as the civil war has been dubbed by historian. And now, he has rebuilt what was broken. The Ridian Empire is restored.

But this is not the Empire of old.

**The Structure of the Restored Ridian Empire**
===============================================

**Imperial Core - The lands directly ruled by Emperor Metilles**
----------------------------------------------------------------

**Myria**

*   The symbolic and political heart of the Empire, now the Imperial Capital.
    

**The Myridon Dynasty**

*   While still retaining their status as a state under the Empire, their power has been severely limited as a result of the War of False Emperor's resolution.
    

**The Stormwatch Duchy**

*   As the primary loyalist force within the Empire, their position has been strengthened, and their territories have been reclaimed in a minor reclamation war against Liberty. Several high-standing loyalist figures got appointed as Lords or Counts of the cities of the Duchy.
    

**The Ridian Empire Remnants**

*   Seeing as the Ridian Empire now is restored, this state has been renamed to Old Ridia, honoring the roots of the Emperor's family line. As with the Stormwatch Duchy, loyalist figures were also raised to nobility, granted lordships or positions at court.
    

**Himari Kingdom**

*   Fully integrated into the Empire while retaining its nobility and structure. The backbone of the Empire’s food and infrastructure. Parts of Rea Eloras were integrated into the Himari Kingdom to provide landed access to Old Ridia.
    

**The Caldian States**

*   Restructured as a maritime protectorate, being the primary source naval cooperation and trade for the Empire.
    

**Unarith Duchy**

*   Maintains its neutrality, and by having remained untouched all throughout the War of False Emperors is currently the most prosperous region of the Empire.
    

**Allied Regions**
------------------

**Rea Eloras**

*   In recognition of the help the Yu'ir Elves of Rea Eloras, granting the Emperor and his troops and the refugees of various battles knowledge of and sanctuary in the Realm of the Planar Tree, and to honor the loss of it, Rea Eloras has been granted its independence from the Empire. To secure a landed access between the Himari Kingdom and Old Ridia, Rea Eloras have given over a small portion of their lands to the Himari Kingdom.
    
*   This was written in the Treaty of Ashen Roots. No future emperor may revoke it, under penalty of exile and divine condemnation.
    
*   They now remain a close and trusted ally of the Empire.
    

**Cultural and Political Reform**
=================================

Emperor Metilles has established a council of representatives from around the Empire. The Council does not rule, but it influences, ensuring the Emperor’s vision never strays from the people’s needs.

The following are the members of the council as of the Emperor's Coronation, 960 A.D.

**Imperial Protector of the Faith**

*   High Warden Thalia
    
    *   High Warden of the Temple of the Everwatch
        
    *   Trusted confidante of Metilles and a voice of restraint.
        
    *   Oversees religious matters, moral guidance, and guardianship oaths across the Empire.
        

**Shield of the Empire**

*   Duke Renval Kaen of the Stormwatch Duchy
    
    *   Lord of Wintergard
        
    *   Veteran commander of the Wavebreakers under Belldam Bhaston, who joined Emperor Metilles after the fall of the Stormwatch Duchy
        
    *   Directs the Empire’s military structure, border defense, and officer academies.
        

**Speaker of the Tides**

*   Merissa Vael
    
    *   Lady of Wandermere
        
    *   Accomplished Admiral, known for winning the naval battle outside Selymbria.
        
    *   Coordinates naval contributions and ensures fair trade, shipping, and coastal defense.
        

**Steward of the Greenlands**

*   Duke Alren of Himari
    
    *   Lord of Sunhold
        
    *   Close political ally to Metilles since before the War of False Emperors. Used his resources during the war primarily to ensure stability within his own realm, and to help those in need, keeping the peace as much as possible.
        
    *   Oversees agricultural stability, trade routes, and rural infrastructure.
        

**Whisper of the Empire**

*   Ser Caldre Myros
    
    *   Former noble-turned-spymaster
        
    *   Once operated in the shadows through diplomacy, manipulation and carefully wielded secrets. Emperor Metilles invited him into the open, as a quiet architect of peace and strategic pressure.
        
    *   Maintains both open and closed relations with other nations. Acts as the Empire’s eyes and ears beyond its borders.
        

**Master Artificer**

*   Nedric Berylriver
    
    *   Former Myridon artificer captured during the fall of Myrida Aeca.
        
    *   Pardoned and elevated by Emperor Metilles due to his unmatched expertise and vision.
        
    *   Oversees magical infrastructure, forge technology, and responsible innovation under observation of the council.
        

**Regional Benefits & Penalties**
---------------------------------

**Stormwatch Duchy**

*   Their lands are restored and rebuilt using Imperial funds; surviving officers are given places of honor in the capital or their own fiefdom as thanks for their resistance during occupation.
    
*   The Wavebreakers and Bloodsworn are now fully absorbed into the imperial army structure, reporting directly to the Shield of the Empire.
    
*   Will be granted the towns of Winterwale, Padstow and Walden from Caldian States territory.
    

**Caldian States**

*   Caldian sailors receive exclusive trade rights in Imperial ports and are given tax-free docking in Himari and Myria. In return for this, they must become more centralized, like the other nations within the Empire.
    
*   Will be forced to concede the towns of Winterwale, Padstow and Walden to Stormwatch Duchy territory.
    

**Himari Kingdom**

*   Forced to send a substantial amount of their resources to the more war-torn places in the Empire, and citizens get increased tax for reconstruction efforts to offset their wealth and prosperity. In return for this, the position of Steward of the Greenlands is granted to a Himari noble.
    

**Myridon Dynasty**

*   Public trials for war crimes; certain nobles who supported The False Emperor Sauntes exiled or imprisoned.
    
*   Cities and civilians who surrendered peacefully were spared retaliation and now benefit from reconstruction aid.
    
*   The False Emperor Sauntes is exiled. All his lands, diminished as they are, go to Sauntes' heir, Caspar Myridon under Imperial oversight.
    

**Myria**

*   False Emperor Liodes, recognizing Emperor Metilles' rightful claim and witnessing his victory firsthand, steps down willingly. He is granted stewardship over the Imperial Archive, where artifacts and written history is kept. He remains beloved by Myria’s people and is respected for preventing further bloodshed.
