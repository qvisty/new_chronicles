Personality
-----------

Selwyn is a quiet person, preferring to listen rather than speak. He is also fiercely loyal to those who he believe actually deserves his loyalty.

He's shown himself to be jealous and ambitious, building up to what happened in Isandor at the betrayal.

He is also very religious, although he doesn't speak openly about his faith a lot.

* * *

Background
----------

Not much is known about Selwyn's actual background. What you would know is that he spent much of his life in a religious order in Myridon, which is where he formed the Black Clover Swords.

Appearance
----------

Selwyn is a rather unassuming man. He stands around 6 feet tall, with a muscular build. He usually wears practical clothes or armor, rather than ones specifically for looks, and is never found without his sword at his side.

Running down the length of his arms, stopping at his hands, is his dragonmark. The green lines of his dragonmark ran around his arm, with a cold green color. However, after unknown events, his dragonmark changed, seemingly corrupted. It turned a dark purple, and dances across his skin like lines of shadow.

The lines also started to look like chains.

Background
----------

Count Strahd von Zarovich is the ruler of all the lands of Barovia. He rules with an iron fist, relying on fear and mysticism to keep the Barovians in line.  
He has been in power for centuries, never wavering, and with very few revolts against him.  
Some call him the Devil, others Master. All are afraid of him.

Appearance
----------

Strahd is pale, looking to be in his forties.  
His jet-black hair is kept swept back from his face, resulting in a prominent brow and widow’s peak.  
His features are gaunt but handsome and his dark red eyes have an intensity that can make even the strongest man nervous.  
His physique is lean but muscular, and he has been noted for his height—well over six feet.  
He tends to dress in exquisite finery, albeit in a somewhat outdated style.
