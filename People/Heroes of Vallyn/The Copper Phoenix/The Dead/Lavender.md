Description / Appearance
------------------------

_Lavender’s first visible feature is her large, mothlike wings. She has wavy brown hair, usually tied up with bangs. her only semi-visible weapon is a dagger strapped to her thigh, and that is usually kept concealed. her right arm is rotted away at the forearm, revealing just bone, with a clawed bone hand._

**HAIR****:Brown, Wavy**

**EYES****: Violet**

**SKIN****:Pale**

Personality Traits
------------------

Lavender is a very deadpan cynic. She assumes the worst in most others, unlike her sister, and is very wary of those she doesn’t know. She likes dark colors, and from a very young age had an interest in the macabre. She sounds a bit like Daria, and is similarly quick with snarky side-comments.
