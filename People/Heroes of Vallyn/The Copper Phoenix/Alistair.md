Description / Appearance
------------------------

_Alistair is an petite androgynous Eladrin, often seen in different stylish outfits. He has curly blonde locks, and brown perfect skin. His jaw is a little more well defined than the picture, and his ears are much much longer and adorned with piercings and fancy chains. He also sports fangs, likely from some disconnected fey beast in his bloodline. he does not have boobs. He wields a jade-colored fan with a gold inlaid design, and a sickle with a similar appearance, both likely passed down through his family. His dragonmark is on his shoulder blades, and depict dragonfly wings, that shimmer faintly in iridescent light when in use._

**HAIR****: Curly, Blonde,**

**EYES****: Dreamy, Emerald**

**SKIN****: Light brown, Freckled**

Personality Traits
------------------

Much like many on the fey court, Alistair is prideful and hard to predict. His Eladrin ancestry dictates certain occasional mood swings. He is loyal to a fault, flirtatious and charming when he wants to be, but doesn’t ever seem to show his entire thought process.

He is a trained and very talented dancer, in multiple different styles.

Background
----------

Not much is known by the party about Alistair's upbringing, His family was revealed to contain influential members on the Fey Court, at least a long time ago. He has said that he grew up on the “east side of Winter,” when asked about his origins. He has hinted that he has been gone from the world for a very long time, the exact number he claims is unknown, but regardless hard to quantify with how time works across planes. He said he just returned in the last three years.

Has recently revealed to the CP that he was likely killed by the blade that caused the merging
