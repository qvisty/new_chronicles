Description / Appearance
------------------------

_Myxi is a short and flexible woman with coal black hair and brown eyes. She as pale white skin, a small nose and ears. In her left ear she has a purple rock earring. Myxi wears an oversized well-worn coat where one side is a light grey colour and the other a dark brown. The coat is held in place by some loose white cloth wrapped around her torso and a wide brown leather waist belt with a blue rope and brass buckle on top. Her pants are a loose fitting and dark grimy green collar with the ends tucked well into a pair of sturdy leather boots and held together with tightly wrapped rope. Under her coat she wears some tight-fitting long-sleeved cloth clothing wrapped with leather straps going all around her body, seemingly to add a constant pressure. On the blue rope of the belt, she has a pouch, a devil mask and a few pieces of different jewellery tied to it. lastly, she has a long white silk scarf around her neck._

Personality Traits
------------------

Myxi looks like she is constantly running/jogging when walking next to someone, and is easily distracted by things she sees around her like fancy architecture, book stores or cute animals. Due to her height she struggles getting to various different things, but she usually gets around it by using her acrobatics to leverage herself up high enough to grab what she wanted. When there is not a lot to do Myxi will usually be found reading a book, practising her dance/sword dance, her magic focus, or playing her flute.

Background
----------

Myxi started traveling about 2 years ago, before her travels she lived in a small village with her loving parents and her friends, practising her skills and learning what she could from books and the people around her.
