Personality Traits
------------------

Canela is intentionally soft. She wants to offer the comfort and peace that her mentor has shown to her when she most needed it, but does not hesitate when a situation calls for a stronger hand. She dedicated herself to loving life, to treasure it and accept the bad together with the good.

Nothing is ever lost in the endless cycles of gain and loss. It’s life.

Canela has accepted who she was before Senna saved her, but she can’t quite forgive herself for what she did yet.

Background
----------

Canela wasn’t allowed to be herself for a very long time. She was unable to experience emotions, followed orders without remorse or hesitation and knew nothing about a possible life outside of that world.

It wasn’t possible. The drugs made sure of that.

But after an incident that left her body shred to pieces, she became useless to the organization. They stopped providing for her, forcing her to go through the painful detox on her own.

Senna, her mentor, saved her. He stole her away, hid her in a safe haven where she had the time to recover, find herself and for the first time be more than an abandoned tool. Thanks to him, she became who she is now and she will value that gift for as long as she lives.
