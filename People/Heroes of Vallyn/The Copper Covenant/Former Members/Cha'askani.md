Personality Traits
------------------

Kani prided herself on her strength. There was an inherent restlessness that appeared to others as reckless but she was willing to do everything to never have something taken from her again. Not without a fight, and boy, did she fight.

She liked to live wild and loud and got uncomfortable if people tried to see more in her than what she showed them. Maybe she hid even from herself but for now it was enough for everyone to perceive her as violent and stubborn. Even Kani wasn’t sure if there’s anything more than that left in her.

Background
----------

At home in the Anuhm Realms, Cha’askani was part of the People of Iyantu, who lived in the valleys and the mountains. The Iyantu are a two class society; the Antasaya, gifted with wings by their gods and the Huayas, those born without wings and therefore of lower status. Cha'askanis father was the brother of the Reign Father and Army General of the Antasaya. She was raised to become a warrior like him.

One day she met a boy of the Huayas on a hunting track and befriended him, finding out that they were born on the same day. Two years later, shortly before Cha'askani would join the ranks of the army they meet once more and the boy attacked Cha'askani. He stole her wings, claiming that she has stolen his fate when they were born.

After the attacka Cha'askani felt too ashamed to return to her family and decided to leave the valleys. It was the first time Kani ran away from anything.
