Description / Appearance
------------------------

Mika has long dirty blond hair that she keeps in three braids a main one that runs down her back and two smaller ones on each side of her head just in front of her horns, each of this braids are held in place with a small cylindrical golden clamp with a bunch of small markings on them. She has two black horns that stick out from the top of her head curing down and around to the side of her head like goat horns. Her eyes have a deep golden yellow color with oval pupils that lay horizontally in the irises. Mika is quite tall for being a dwarf standing at an impressive 145cm but her weight is even more impressive weighing 101kg. Mika has never broken a bone in her life and she claims that her bones are made of iron just like her maul. She is also very strong and has good muscle definition but still has a bit of fat on her body. (if she flexes she has a six pack)

Mika wears a black leather bodysuit with long arms that end in a sort of half glove, and a pair of black leather shorts. On top of that she wears a sudo skirt made with a thick leather and fur with openings on both sides, Thick leather bracers on each arm, a pair of sturdy high boots and a large bear skin "cape" on her shoulders secured with a small steel chain. Under her cape she has a "large" backpack/bag filled with all her stuff.

Personality Traits
------------------

Mika tends to be a fairly happy person, eager to explore and spend time with her friends or spend it making new friends. She is also a perfectionist at heart at least when it comes to her crafts. Over time she has learned that everything can't be perfect, and that is ok. It just means you can get better at it, but this means there is constructive criticism or observations to be made, and if she can't get that, then she becomes frustrated. If the thing she made is not perfect there should be something she can learn from it but if she can't learn anything from it she feels like she failed imperfectly or simply failed failing.

Mika is a very curious person, but not a very bright one, leading her to often touch or interact with things that's possibly or obviously dangerous. This would be bad if Mika was not so lucky. A trait that she claims, she has to do with a shiny stone she carries with her at all times. A stone she randomly found while on the road to the capital. Her wery one Lucky rock. (+1 Stone of Good Luck)

She also struggles with expressing her emotions not really knowing what she is feeling herself a lot of the time. And afraid of being rejected or laughed at for not understanding them or for doing/feeling something "wrong" she usually doesn't talk about them in any meaningful or deep way.

Background
----------

Mika worked in a smith's shop with her papap Haldovon in a small village in the outskirts of Myridon. She would help make basic tools the villagers ordered, and make the deliveries. Mika would also travel to the village center in her free time to talk with people and learn the crafts from a bunch of different people. This made her quite knowledgeable about different crafts but she also have not had any formal schooling and at best had lessons that revolve around one of her crafts and selling them.

Other than that Holdovon would spar with her and teach Mika how to fight and weld a plethora of different weapons, but the Maul was always her favorite to fight with.

All the things Mika has talked and sheard so far have all been stories from when she seemingly was an adult, and if asked Mika would either just pretend she did not hear the question or answer it by deflecting to another thing and talking about that.
