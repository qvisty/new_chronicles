Personality Traits
------------------

She is quiet when meeting new people, but tends to make friends relatively easily. She likes to have fun and doesn't mind getting in trouble, but she likes to make sure it's relatively safe to do so, and if it isn't she makes sure she's along for it in case any accidents happen. She tries to be better but it's difficult when considering where she spent her formative years. She prays often, especially after doing something that she believes Helm would disapprove of

Background
----------

She was in a band of mercenaries that were hired to do odd jobs around the fallen empire, along the way she was saved by a cleric of Helm and decided that she wants to be able to protect and save others around her as they had.
