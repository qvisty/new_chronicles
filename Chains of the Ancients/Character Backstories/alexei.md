Alexei Rustborn: Aka The White Fox

Alexei was born to two talented Wood Elven mages, Vulred and Natasia Addan in Enya\. Not that she remembers them, anyway\. They rose to political prominence by providing information on human caravans providing materials to settlers, allowing the elves to intercept the materials, inconveniencing the humans attempting to encroach on historic elven homeland\.

Eventually, The couple was discovered to be double agents, providing insider information about Elven troop movements to their informant, a dwarven crime lord named Tazna Rustborn, colloquially called The Boar, running a gang called the Beastial Enclave\. They were thrown in a holding cell for questioning when Alexei was just a baby\. Natasia and Vuldren were murdered in custody, whether by a goon sent by Tazna, a crooked guard, or something else entirely\. 

Perhaps feeling some form of guilt, Tazna took Alexei in and fled to the Myridian Empire, starting a new base of operations with a newborn\.

Tazna was quick to tell anyone that she was in no way a mother, and told Alexei her parent’s fate as soon as she was old enough to understand, repeatedly saying “I’m not ya mom, kid\.” Nevertheless, she was protective of her like a daughter \(or a trophy,\) and she loved her like a mother, even introducing herself as a Rustborn\.

Tazna, admittedly not the best mother, and would show her darker side at times when upset, for the most part showing her love, or something that looked like it to Alexei, also taught her the art of stealth and hand to hand combat, putting her to work as early as 7, mostly using her as a cute decoy\. Then, aged 21, she was trusted to perform heists and assassinations all on her own, wearing a white mask and earning a colloquial name of The White Fox and officially entering The Bestial Enclave

Some 40 years laters, she was discovered by the Myridian government\. She was thrown in jail for a life sentence, with officials promising an early release if she acted as a mole against the mysterious crime lord she appeared to be working for\. Alexei refused to rat out the only family she had ever known\. She stayed in jail for 15  odd years not feeling like much to an Elf, before being offered another, more peculiar deal\. work with the military, secure the future of her home, and walk a free woman\. 

Slip up and get put down like a dog\. The rest is history\.
